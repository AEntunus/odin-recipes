Tasty, Delicious Beef Goulash

You should try this goulash if you haven't.

Have you tried this goulash yet?

Ingredients:

  • Beef (the fattier, the better) - 450-700g
  • Garlic cloves - 3-4
  • Butter
  • Potatoes - 3-4 large
  • Carrots - 4-5
  • Piquillo peppers - 2-3
  • Onion
  • Diced tomatoes (canned) - 100g
  • Ground red pepper (whichever floats your boat)
  • Ground coriander - 1tbs
  • Bay leaf - 3-4
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
  1. Cut meat into chunks, season w/ salt, pepper & let rest
  2. Peel potatoes, carrots and prep rest of veggies
  3. Heat up butter, mix in red pepper, coat meat in the mixture and cover w/ hot water
  4. Whilst meat is cooking, chop up prepped vegetables
  5. Add everything to the pot, cover and simmer for 3-4 hours
  6. Enjoy.
