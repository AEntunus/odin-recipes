Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti - 110-140g
  • Garlic cloves - 5-7
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
    • Chili flakes (optional)
    • Fresh parsley (optional)
  1. Bring water to a boil, make sure to salt heavily
  2. Cook pasta until al dente
  3. Bringe olive oil to sizzle and add finely chopped garlic
  4. Sizzle garlic, don't burn
  5. Add chili flakes
  6. Add pasta and toss until coated
  7. Garnish w/ freshley chopped parsley
